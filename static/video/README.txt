SafeDrive — hero video carousel
================================

The landing-page hero is a 3-slide carousel. Each slide shows a cinematic
photo poster out of the box, and upgrades to full-motion video the moment you
drop matching .mp4 files here:

    static/video/hero1.mp4
    static/video/hero2.mp4
    static/video/hero3.mp4

Recommended clips (muted, looping, 8–15s, 1080p, H.264 .mp4):
  hero1 — city traffic / headlights at night
  hero2 — passenger in the back seat looking at a phone
  hero3 — driver's POV on a road / highway

Where to get free, license-clear footage:
  • https://mixkit.co/free-stock-video/  (search "city traffic", "car")
  • https://www.pexels.com/videos/
  • https://coverr.co/

Keep each file small (ideally < 6 MB) so the page stays fast. Until you add
them, the high-resolution photo posters display automatically — no broken UI.
